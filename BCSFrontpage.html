A Little About This Tutorial

This tutorial is aimed to teach you how to design and create your own experiment. It is set up in several sections. We have the general experiment design track, which should be the main track you follow. There is also a parallel track that explains the paradigm, Self-Paced Reading (SPR). You can follow the yellow buttons on the top and through the navigation for the SPR track.

After the general part of the tutorial, there is an optional quiz. We highly recommend you take these quizzes. They will test to make sure you understand what you read, but will also be able to guide you with the materials you do not understand.

This is a Learn More Box. It will have extra information!

For any items that are highlighted you can click on them and a pop-up will appear on the side. This will include defintiions, helpful links, other comments.

Self-Paced Reading Specific

As you go through this tutorial you will have the option of going through the self-paced reading (SPR) specific parallel tutorial. We suggest you go through the general tutorial first and then after go through the SPR specific tutorial. The navigation bar is coded so that you can reference back any section that you need.
